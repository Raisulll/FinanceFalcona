Careers at Falcona Finance

At Falcona Finance, we are always on the lookout for passionate, talented individuals who are eager to contribute to the success of our clients. If you have a drive for excellence, a strong work ethic, and a desire to make a meaningful impact, we would love to hear from you. Join our dynamic team and build a rewarding career in the financial services industry.

Contact Us

1. Why Work with Us?

+
-

At Falcona Finance, we value our employees and believe in fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment. We offer opportunities for growth and development, competitive salaries, and a work-life balance that allows you to thrive both personally and professionally. As part of our team, you’ll be part of a company that prioritizes innovation, integrity, and client-focused solutions.

2. Open Positions

+
-

We are currently looking to fill the following positions:

  • Junior Accountant – Responsible for assisting in the preparation of financial statements, tax filings, and general accounting tasks. Apply now
  • Senior Tax Consultant – Focus on advising clients on tax planning strategies and ensuring compliance with tax laws. Apply now
  • Business Analyst – Provide strategic insights, financial forecasting, and operational analysis to clients. Apply now
  • Payroll Specialist – Manage payroll processing, CRA remittances, and employee benefits for clients. Apply now

3. How to Apply

+
-

To apply for any of the positions listed, please follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Review the job description and ensure your qualifications align with the requirements.
  • Step 2: Submit your resume and cover letter through our online application form.
  • Step 3: If shortlisted, you will be contacted for an interview. We look for individuals who share our values and demonstrate passion for their work.

If you are interested in a role but do not see the right fit listed, feel free to send your resume and cover letter for future opportunities to careers@falconafinance.ca.

4. Employee Benefits

+
-

At Falcona Finance, we offer a comprehensive benefits package designed to support our employees' health, wellness, and financial security. Our benefits include:

  • Competitive salary and performance bonuses
  • Health and dental insurance
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Paid time off and vacation days
  • Professional development and training opportunities
  • Employee wellness programs

5. About Falcona Finance

+
-

Falcona Finance is a trusted chartered accountancy firm providing comprehensive financial services to individuals and businesses across Canada. We specialize in tax planning, financial reporting, audit services, business advisory, and more. Our team is committed to helping clients navigate the complexities of the financial world with confidence and expertise.

As an employee at Falcona Finance, you’ll be joining a team of professionals who are passionate about delivering top-tier service and creating lasting value for our clients. We are committed to professional growth, collaboration, and the well-being of our staff.
