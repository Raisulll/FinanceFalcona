CRA Representation & Tax Dispute Resolution

Facing audits, disputes, or penalties with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) can be stressful. We provide expert CRA representation, advocating on your behalf and assisting with audits, tax disputes, and negotiations. Our goal is to protect your rights and ensure you remain fully compliant with Canadian tax regulations.

  • Professional advocacy in tax disputes
  • Audit defense and support
  • Assistance with CRA negotiations
  • Minimizing penalties and liabilities
  • Guidance through tax compliance issues

Tax Compliance for Non-Residents with Canadian Income

Non-residents earning income in Canada must comply with Canadian tax laws. We offer specialized tax compliance services for non-residents, focusing on rental income, property sales, and other Canadian income. Our team is knowledgeable in filing Section 216 tax returns and handling other non-resident tax obligations.

  • Expertise in non-resident tax laws
  • Tax filing for rental income and property sales
  • Compliance with Section 216 for non-resident tax returns
  • Risk minimization and penalty avoidance
  • Comprehensive tax planning for non-residents
