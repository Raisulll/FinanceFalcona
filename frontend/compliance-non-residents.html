Our Approach to
Tax Compliance for Non-Residents with Canadian Income

Non-residents earning income in Canada face unique tax obligations. At Falcona Finance, we offer specialized tax compliance services for non-residents, ensuring that rental income, property sales, and other Canadian earnings are accurately reported and compliant with Canadian tax laws. Our team is well-versed in Section 216 tax returns and non-resident tax obligations, providing the expertise needed to navigate these complexities.

Tax Filing for Rental Income and Property Sales

+
-

Our services include tax filing for non-residents earning rental income or engaging in property sales within Canada. We ensure proper reporting of these incomes, ensuring you meet Canadian tax regulations and take advantage of any eligible deductions, while avoiding unnecessary penalties.

Compliance with Section 216 for Non-Resident Tax Returns

+
-

We provide expertise in filing Section 216 tax returns for non-residents. This section applies to individuals who earn rental income in Canada, and our team ensures that all necessary forms are filed correctly and timely, optimizing your tax position under the Canadian tax system.
